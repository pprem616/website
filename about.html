BUDT748 INDUSTRY PRACTICUM
BUDT748: Industry Practicum is a capstone course for graduate students at the University of Maryland's Robert H. Smith School of Business. In this hands-on course, students work with a company on a real-world project to solve a specific business problem using technology.
